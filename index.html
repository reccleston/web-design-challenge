Welcome to AsteroidSpot!

AsteroidSpot is the number one place to get the question that's on everyone's mind answered: How many asteroids have almost hit earth in the past year? With data served from the NASA Asteroids NeoWs (Near Earth Objects) Open API. Provided are scatter plots depicting an asteroid's astronomically high velocity as compared to its size and passing proximity to Earth. As this dataset dates back to 1900, only the first week of 2020, 2019, 2018, and 2017 were examined to get the faintest taste of what goes on just at our doorstep.

Hope you enjoy!
